5. Kunci jawaban lks bahasa inggris kelas 5. Hal 18


Berikut bentuk negative dari kalimat tersebut.

1. (+) I read a book yesterday.

(-) I did not read a book yesterday.

2. (+) I went to the market yesterday.

(-) I did not go to the market yesterday.

3. (+) We studied English last week.

(-) We did not study English last week.

4. (+) They played basketball this morning.

(-) They did not play basketball this morning.

5. (+) My mother cooked chicken soup yesterday.

(-) My mother did not cook chicken soup yesterday.

6. (+) Jasmine visited the zoo last holiday.

(-) Jasmine did not visit the zoo last holiday.

7. (+) I was a student.

(-) I was not a student.

8. (+) I did my homework.

(-) I did not do my homework.

9. (+) They were tired.

(-) They were not tired.

10. (+) They were happy.

(-) They were not happy.

Penjelasan:

Simple past tense ialah bentuk kalimat dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Struktur Kalimat

1. Struktur kalimat verbal, struktur kalimat yang predikatnya adalah kata kerja.

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did + not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) He bought a book yesterday.

(-) He did not buy a book yesterday.

(?) Did he buy a book yesterday?

2. Struktur kalimat nominal, struktur kalimat yang predikatnya bukan kata kerja.

(+) S + to be (was/ were) + O

(-) S + to be (was/ were) + not + O

(?) To be (was/ were) + S + O + ?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) She was a doctor.

(-) She was not a doctor.

(?) Was she a doctor?

24. kunci jawaban lks bahasa inggris kelas 9 semester 1 hal 10-14​


Raka: Well (1) done, Donny. Your painting is the best at our school. I am sure, our school will send you as the (2) representative for the national painting contest.

Donny: Thanks a million to say that. The other paintings are good also.

Raka: Yes but yours is the best. You painted very (3) well.

Donny: (4) Thanks a lot. Please give me support if I am sent to the national painting contest.

Raka: Of course. We are best friend. We should (5) support each other.

Donny: That's right. Thanks again.

Raka: Don't mention it.

Pembahasan

1. Whose painting are they talking about?

Answer: They are talking about Donny's painting.

2. Why does Raka congratulate Donny?

Answer: Raka congratulates Donny because Donny's painting is the best at their school.

3. Does Raka always support Donny?

Answer: Yes, he does.

4. Find the expression of congratulating in the dialogue!

Answer: "Well done, Donny."

5. What does Donny say to respond Raka?

Answer: Donny says, "Thanks a million to say that."
