17. Only for geniuses. who can Answer


2 x 3 = 6
3 x 4 = 12
4 x 5 = 20
5 x 6 = 30
6 x 7 = 42
9 x 10 = 90

Maaf kalo salah Diketahui :
U2 = 6 = 2 × 3 = 2 (2 + 1)
U3 = 12 = 3 × 4 = 3(3 + 1)
U4 = 20 = 4 × 5 = 4(4 + 1)
Un = n(n + 1)


Ditanya :
U9?

Jawab :
Un = n(n + 1)
U9 = 9(9 + 1)
U9 = 9(10)
U9 = 90
