17. Contoh cerita menggunakan simple past tense past continous tense dan past perfect tense


Pembahasan:


Sebelum membuat cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense, kita sebaiknya mengetahui struktur kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense terlebih dahulu. Berikut penjelasannya.



Simple past tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang dilakukan pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + didn't / did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I watched the World Cup on TV last night.



Past continuous tense

Tenses ini digunakan menyatakan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + was / were + V-ing + O

(-) S + wasn't / weren't + V-ing + O

(?) Was / were + S + V-ing + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I was cooking dinner when you called me yesterday


Past perfect tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi sebelum kejadian lain pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O +?

Contoh kalimat: She had had breakfast before she went to school.

29. pengertian simple past tense beserta contoh


Simple Past adalah Tenses yg menunjukkan kejadian/peristiwa di waktu/masa lampau dan selesai di masa/waktu lampau juga.

Simple Past yg berbentuk Verbal (yg ada kata kerja/verb)

(+) Subject + V2 + Object

= I play a ball yesterday

(Saya bermain sebuah bola kemarin)

(-) S + did not + V1 + Object

= I did not play a ball yesterday

(Saya tidak bermain bola kemarin)

(?) did I play a ball yesterday

(Apa saya bermain sebuah bola kemarin?)

Simple Past bentuk Nominal (yg tak ada kerja/ tanpa verb)

(+) Subject + was/were + adjective/adverb/noun

= I was so happy yesterday  

(saya sangat bahagia kemarin)

(-) S + was/were + not + object (adj/adv/noun)

= I was not happy yesterday

(Saya tak bahagia kemarin)

(?) was/were + S + O (adj/adv/noun)

= was I happy yesterday?

Time signal (waktu yg menunjukkan Past Tense)

= yesterday, last, ago dll

36. Tulis rumus simple past tense dan simple present tense Beserta Contohnya


Simple past tense:

subject + V2 + O

ex:
(+) I went to primary school in England.
(-) My grandmother didn't like cats.
(?) Did you get my letter?


Simple present tense:

Subject + V(use s at the back if the subject are he, she, it) + O

ex:
(+) She wears jeans all the time.
(-) He doesn't enjoy it.
(?) Does Jack smoke?@Answer:

1) Simple Present
(+) S + V1(s/es) + O/C => She reads a book.
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C => She doesn't read a book.
(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O/C? => Does she read a book?

2) Simple Past
(+) S + V2 + O/C => We cook a cake.
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O/C => We didn't cook a cake.
(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C? => Did we cook a cake?
