15. Bahasa Inggris hal 74 kelas 8tolong di bantu​


Complete the sentences using "it" or "them" !

3. Thinneke : Why not? Why you don't invite them?

4. Mom : Put them on the table, please.

5. Roemi : Throw them away.

6. Aprillia : Would you like to do it now?

7. Arni : Let's tidy it now.

8. Lela : Wait! Don't touch it.

9. Intas : Let's eat them.

10. Yudi : I'm painting it.

Pembahasan

Perbedaan antara "it" dan "them"

Untuk " it " , termasuk ke dalam golongan kata subject tunggal. Umumnya penggunaan kata " it " seperti menunjukkan sesuatu (itu) yang jumlahnya hanya satu (tunggal).

Sedangkan, untuk " them " (mereka) bukanlah jenis subject, melainkan object dengan bentuk jamak (banyak).

___________

• Pengertian Subject

Merupakan kata utama dalam memulai pembicaraan atau dialog.

• Pengertian Object

Merupakan kata yang umumnya digunakan atau di letakkan pada pola tempat akhir atau setelah dari predikat.

Misalnya seperti grammar, kata subject selalu diletakkan pada kata depan (utama). Berbeda dengan object yang diletakkan di akhir. Example :

Simple past tense :

(+) S + V2 + -ed/-d + O.(-) S + did + not + V1 + O.(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Simple present tense :

(+) S + V1 + -es/-s + O.(-) S + does + V1 + O.(?) Does + S + V1 + O + ?

Present continuous tense :

(+) S + am/is/are + V1 + ing + O.(-) S + am/is/are + not + V1 + ing + O.(?) Am/is/are + S + V1 + ing + O + ?

Dan masih banyak lagi yang menggunakan penempatan seperti itu lainnya antara subject dan object.

25. jawaban paket bahasa inggris kelas 9 halaman 74 dan 75 kurikulum 2013


1.) Written: Measure rice with the measuring cup. Then, rinse the rice thoroughly using other container.

Spoken: First, measure rice with the measuring cup. Then, rinse the rice thoroughly using other container.

2.) Written: Put the rinsed rice in the pan and add water to the recommend scale or to your personal taster.

Spoken: Put the rinsed rice in the pan and then add water to the recommended scale or to your personal taster.

3.) Written: Soak the rice in water at least for 30 minutes.

Spoken: After that, soak the rice water at least for 30 minutes.

4.) Written: Place the pan into the body. Make sure the pan settles properly.

Spoken: Next, place the pan into the body and make sure that the pan settles properly.

5.) Written: Close the lid firmly.

Spoken: After that, you can close the lid firmly.

6.) Written: Plug in and press the switch. The light indicating cook will be on. The cooking will start immediately.

Spoken: You have to plug in the pan and press the switch button. Then, the light indicating “COOK” will be on. It means the cooking will start immediately.

7.) Spoken: When the rice is cooked, the switch will move up to keep the it warm, and then the light indicating “WARM” will be on.

After that, you can leave the lid closed for at least fifteen minutes so the rice will be fully steamed.

After it’s done, continue to scoop and mix the rice well.

8.) Written: Unplug

Spoken: Finally, it’s done. Now, you can unplug the pan. Don’t forget to do it carefully.

Pembahasan

Associating method atau dalam bahasa Indonesia dikenal dengan langkah-langkah melakukan sesuatu ialah sebuah tahapan yang perlu dilakukan untuk melakukan suatu pekerjaan. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, biasanya kalimat ini terdapat dalam teks prosedur.

Judul: Nasib Hutan Kita Semakin Suram

Sumber: Blog Ruang Guru

Paragraf 1 :
