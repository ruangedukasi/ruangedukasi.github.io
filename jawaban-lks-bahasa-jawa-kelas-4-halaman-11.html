22. Lks bahasa inggris kelas 8 halaman 9-11


The (5) creation (c) of our Solar System took place billions of years before there were any people around to witness it. Our own (6) evolution (h) is tied closely to the evolution of the Solar System. Thus, without understanding from where the Solar System came from, it is difficult to comprehend how mankind came to be (7) made up (a) believe that the Solar System evolved from a giant cloud of dust and gas. They believe that this dust and gas began to collapse under the weight of its own gravity. As it did so, the matter contained within this could begin moving in a giant (8) circle (i) much like the water in a drain moves around the center of the drain in a circle. At the (9) center (e) of this spinning cloud, a small star began to form. This star grew larger and larger as it collected more and more of the dust and gas that collapsed into it. Further away from the center of this mass where the star was forming, there were smaller clumps of dust and gas that were also collapsing. The star in the center eventually ignited forming our Sun, while the smaller clumps became the planets, minor (10) planets (j), moons, comets, and asteroids.

Remidial

1. My whole family go to the grandparents' village once a year in other town.

2. Rain seldom falls in the Sahara.

3. Leap year comes every four years.

4. The children leave at 8:30 every morning of the week.

5. The weather gets very cold in Helsinki, Finland in the winter.

6. Many birds of Europe fly south to Africa every winter.

7. What will you do if she comes late?

8. What Sarif Hermansyah usually has for breakfast?

9. Your train leaves at 04.25 p.m from platform 2.

10. What Evita does? She's a student.

Ten sentences using form Simple Present Tense:

1. I go to bed at 10 pm every night.

2. Lina goes to school at 6.30 am every morning.

3. My cat always sleeps on my coach.

4. This car is an old car.

5. My uncle always visit us every month.

6. We have dinner at 6 pm.

7. I love my parents.

8. I go to English club with my friends every Saturday.

9. The train always passes this road at 7 am.

10. A cat always loves nap.

Pembahasan

Di dalam soal di atas kita mempelajari dan mengulang kembali tentang Simple Present Tense. Tenses tersebut adalah tenses yang paling mudah dalam bahasa Inggris dan paling dasar, sehingga dengan menguasainya pasti kamu akan bisa menguasai tenses yang lainnya.
