2. perbedaan advertisement pamphlet brochure


Pembahasan:


Berikut adalah penjelasan mengenai perbedaan advertisement, pamphlet, dan brochure.


Advertisement

Advertisement dalam Bahasa Indonesia berarti iklan. Iklan adalah suatu media yang digunakan untuk mempromosikan sesuatu, baik barang maupun jasa. Iklan terbagi atas 2, yaitu iklan cetak atau iklan elektronik.

Iklan cetak adalah iklan yang menggunakan kertas sebagai medianya. Contohnya: brosur, poster, pamflet, spanduk, surat kabar, dan majalah.

Iklan elektronik adalah iklan yang menggunakan alat elektronik sebagai medianya.

Contohnya: radio dan TV


Jadi, kesimpulannya Brochure dan pamphlet termasuk kedalam advertisement. Keduanya berupa iklan cetak.


Selanjutnya perbedaan brochure dan pamphlet.

1. Brochure

Brochure dalam bahasa indonesia berarti brosur. Brosur adalah salah satu bentuk iklan cetak berupa selembar kertas berukuran A4 yang dicetak 2 sisi dan dapat dilipat atau dijilid serta digunakan untuk mempromosikan sesuatu secara detail.


2. Pamphlet

Pamphlet dalam bahasa indonesia berarti pamflet. Pamflet adalah salah satu jenis iklan cetak berupa selembar kertas yang dicetak pada 1 sisi atau 2 sisi dan biasanya digunakan untuk memberi edukasi atau menghibur penerima sekaligus mempromosikan sesuatu.

3. 5 contoh soal pilihan ganda "cause and effect" dan "advertisement"


1.Stalagmites are formed by …

(A) drops of water which enter through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

2. Sinkholes are …

(A) the decorative dripstone features found in caves.

(B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(C) colorful layers of mineral deposits.

(D) None of the above

Jawaban : (B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

3. Which speleothem grows upward from the floor?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Sinkholes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (A) Stalagmites

4. An “inclined ceiling” is one which …

(A) is straight.

(B) is crooked.

(C) is slanted.

(D) is wet.

Jawaban : (C) is slanted.

5. Which of the following are NOT caused by dripping water?

(A) Stalactites

(B) Stalagmites

(C) Slopes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (C) Slopes

25. buatlah conclusion leaflet, brochure, and banner​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Conclusion adalah bagian akhir dari leaflet, brochure, atau banner yang dirancang untuk memberikan kesimpulan dari informasi yang telah disampaikan sebelumnya. Berikut adalah contoh conclusion :

Contoh conclusion untuk leaflet:

In conclusion, we hope this leaflet has provided you with valuable information about our new product. If you have any questions or would like to place an order, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Terjemahan: Kesimpulannya, kami berharap brosur ini memberikan informasi berharga tentang produk baru kami. Jika Anda memiliki pertanyaan atau ingin memesan, jangan ragu untuk menghubungi kami.

Contoh conclusion untuk brochure:

In summary, we believe that our services can help you achieve your goals and take your business to the next level. Thank you for considering our company, and we look forward to working with you.

Terjemahan: Secara keseluruhan, kami percaya bahwa layanan kami dapat membantu Anda mencapai tujuan dan membawa bisnis Anda ke level berikutnya. Terima kasih telah mempertimbangkan perusahaan kami, dan kami berharap dapat bekerja sama dengan Anda.

Contoh conclusion untuk banner:

To conclude, don't miss out on this exclusive offer! Visit our store today and take advantage of our amazing deals.

Terjemahan: Untuk menutup, jangan lewatkan penawaran eksklusif ini! Kunjungi toko kami hari ini dan manfaatkan penawaran hebat kami.

Here are the explanations for the terms related to a "brochure" in English:

1. **Definition of Brochure:**

- A brochure is a printed piece of promotional material or information that is typically folded into a compact format. It is used to provide details about a product, service, event, or organization to the intended audience.

2. **Types of Brochure:**

- Brochures come in various types, including:

- **Bi-Fold Brochure:** A brochure folded in half, creating four panels.

- **Tri-Fold Brochure:** Folded into thirds, typically with six panels.

- **Gate-Fold Brochure:** Folded on both sides, opening like a gate to reveal the content.

- **Z-Fold Brochure:** Zigzag folded into three sections, resembling the letter 'Z.'

- **Accordion-Fold Brochure:** Similar to a Z-fold but with more panels.

- **Booklet or Catalog:** Multiple pages bound together, usually with staples or stitching.

3. **Forms of Brochure:**

- Brochures can serve various purposes, such as:

- **Promotional Brochure:** Used for advertising products or services.

- **Educational Brochure:** Provides information and facts about a topic.

- **Event Brochure:** Used to promote and detail an upcoming event.

- **Corporate Brochure:** Showcases a company's profile, history, and achievements.

- **Non-profit Brochure:** Used by charitable organizations to convey their mission.

- **Travel Brochure:** Offers information about travel destinations and packages.

- **Product Brochure:** Focuses on a specific product's features and benefits.

4. **Examples of Brochures:**

- Examples of brochures include travel brochures for popular tourist destinations, product brochures for new gadgets, educational brochures about health and wellness, and event brochures for conferences or festivals.

5. **Structure of a Brochure:**

- The structure of a brochure typically includes:

- **Cover:** Features an eye-catching design and the brochure's title.

- **Inside Panels:** Contain the main content, including text and images.

- **Headings and Subheadings:** Organize information into sections.

- **Images and Graphics:** Enhance visual appeal and illustrate key points.

- **Contact Information:** Provides details for inquiries or purchases.

- **Call to Action:** Encourages readers to take a specific action, such as visiting a website or making a call.

- **Fold Lines:** Indicate where the brochure should be folded for readability.

Remember, the specific content and design of a brochure can vary widely based on its purpose and target audience.
