17. What are the strategies to answer the question about Main idea, stated details, and unstated details of reading comprehension in TOEFL test? Pliss bgt dijawab yaa kakak


Jawaban:

When answering questions about main ideas, stated details, and unstated details in the TOEFL reading comprehension section, it is important to employ effective strategies to improve your accuracy and efficiency. Here are some strategies you can use:

1. Skim the passage: Before diving into the questions, take a quick glance at the passage to get an overall understanding of the topic, main ideas, and the structure of the text. Pay attention to headings, subheadings, and the first and last sentences of each paragraph.

2. Read the question carefully: Make sure you understand what the question is asking for. Look for keywords that indicate whether the question is about the main idea, stated details, or unstated details. Underline or circle the important keywords in the question.

3. Locate relevant information: Go back to the passage and locate the relevant section or paragraph that contains the information needed to answer the question. Skim that section again to refresh your memory and focus on the specific details being asked.

4. Analyze the main idea: For main idea questions, identify the central theme or purpose of the passage. Look for recurring ideas, key arguments, or the author's main point. Eliminate answer choices that are too specific or unrelated to the overall message of the passage.

5. Identify stated details: For questions about stated details, carefully compare the information in the question with the information in the passage. Look for direct statements, facts, figures, or examples that match the details being asked. Be cautious of answer choices that may sound similar but do not accurately reflect the information presented.

6. Infer unstated details: For questions about unstated details, use your inference and critical thinking skills to make logical deductions based on the information provided in the passage. Look for implied meanings, relationships, or conclusions that can be drawn from the given information. Be careful not to make assumptions or go beyond what is supported by the passage.

7. Eliminate incorrect answer choices: Use the process of elimination to narrow down your options. Cross out answer choices that are clearly incorrect or do not align with the information in the passage. This increases your chances of selecting the correct answer.

8. Use context clues: Pay attention to the context surrounding the stated or unstated details. Look for clue words, transitional phrases, or signals that indicate the author's intention or the relationship between different parts of the passage.

9. Practice with sample questions: Familiarize yourself with different types of reading comprehension questions by practicing with TOEFL sample questions or past papers. This helps you become more comfortable with the question format and develop effective strategies.

Remember to manage your time effectively during the test and allocate enough time to read the passage thoroughly and answer the questions. Regular practice and exposure to a variety of reading passages will also improve your comprehension skills and enhance your ability to identify main ideas, stated details, and unstated details accurately.

35. beri 20 contoh toefl beserta jawaban​


1. … Jane has an excellent resume, she hasn’t found a job yet.

Jawab: Although

Keyword: has; hasn’t.

Pembahasan:

• However: (meskipun) jika di depan kalimat, kalimat tersebut hanya terdiri dari satu klausa.

• Yet: (namun) penggunaan sama dengan however.

• That: (bahwa) berfungsi menerangkan benda atau klausa di depannya.

• Although : (meskipun) berfungsi menggabungkan dua klausa berlawanan.

2. … several universities that have excellent engineering schools.

Jawab: There are

Keyword: several.

Pembahasan: Karena benda yang dirujuk jamak (several universities), frasa yang paling sesuai untuk menerangkan benda tersebut adalah “there are”.

There are + noun jamak – There is + noun tunggal

3. Many Middle Eastern diplomats still feel that the United States is intent … the ultimate police in the region.

Jawab: on being

Keyword: intent.

Pembahasan: Dari keempat jawaban, kata/frasa yang paling memungkinkan untuk mengikuti intent adalah “on being”. Pilihan (A) (to being) salah karena setelah to pasti Verb infinitive. Pilihan (B) (being) salah karena selain berfungsi sebagai clause modifier atau participle, being tidak bisa berdiri sendiri. Pilihan (C) (be) salah karena di depan “be” tidak ada preposisi “to”.

4. Woodrow Wilson believed the United States’ entry into World War I would put a … to the war in months.

Jawab: Stop

Keyword: a.

Pembahasan: Determiner (a/an/ the) + kata benda (noun).

Dari keempat pilihan yang merupakan benda adalah stop (pemberhentian).

5. … of New York’s Erie Canal greatly enhanced trade in the upstate region.

Jawab: The completion

Keyword: of.

Pembahasan: Sebelum preposisi “of” pasti noun (kata benda), dari keempat jawaban tersebut yang merupakan noun adalah “the completion”

6. A leech, after … the skin, is best removed by the application of either salt or heat.

Jawab: it attaches itself to

Keyword: after.

Pembahasan: Kata “after” sebagai preposisi (kata depan) + Gerund (Ving), Kata “after” sebagai conjunction (penghubung) + klausa (subyek+predikat). Jawaban yang paling sesuai adalah klausa “it attaches itself to” karena setelah rumpang masih ada noun (the skin).

7. … east of the Mississippi River.

Jawab: Indigo was usually grown

Keyword: east of the Mississippi River.

Pembahasan:

• Satu klausa terdiri dari paling tidak satu subjek dan satu predikat.

• Pada soal tersebut belum ada keduanya jadi jawabannya pasti subyek + predikat.

• Penyesuaian struktur kalimatnya (pasif: be + V3).

• Penambahan adverb of frequency (peletakannya di antara to be dan V3).

8. … wrote the operetta Babes in Toyland, drawn from the childhood characters of Mother Goose.

Jawab: It was Victor Herbert who

Keyword: wrote the operetta.

Pembahasan: Kata kunci “wrote” merupakan kata kerja (verb).

Kemungkinan struktur kalimat:

Subject + Predicate (wrote)

Subyek + Predicate + relative pronoun (who) + verb (wrote)

9. Some of the oldest and most widespread creation myths are … involving the all-giving “Earth Mother.”

Jawab: those

Keyword: myths.

Pembahasan: Demonstrative yang paling sesuai adalah “those” karena mengacu pada “myths” yang indefinite plural.

40. Apa kegunaan TOEFL??? :-)


TOEFLmerupakan indikator untuk memastikan teman-teman yang berasal bukan dari negara yang menggunakan bahasa Inggris, agar dapat mengikuti perkuliahan dengan baik di negara yang menggunakan bahasa Inggris.digunakan untuk memastikan :

*bahwa mahasiswa tersebut memahami uraian yang diberikan dosen dalam bahasa Inggris (listening skill).
*Mahasiswa memahami buku-buku textbook yang diwajibkan (reading skill)
*Mahasiswa mampu membuat tulisan ilmiah dengan tatabahasa yang benar (writing & grammar skill).
