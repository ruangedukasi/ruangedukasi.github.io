24. jawaban buku mandiri bahasa Inggris kelas 8 halaman 94 Pactice 1​


Write was or were in the blank spaces in the following passage.

Pada pertanyaan berikut kita diminta untuk menuliskan was atau were pada bagian yang kosong.

Pembahasan

Last month we were so excited beause we went on a vacation. It was a beautiful Saturday afternoon and the sun was really bright. Mum, dad, Marina and I were at Soekarno Hatta airport. The flight was at three in the afternoon. The airport was crowded and there were so many people who were waiting for their flight too. After waiting for a few hours, we were on the plane and ready to go to Yogyakarta. We were there for 5 days. It was the best vacation ever.

Untuk mengerjakan soal tersebut, perhatikan penggunaan subyeknya.

Penggunaan Was adalah untuk subyek berjumlah satu (I, She, He, It).Penggunaan Were apabila jumlah subyeknya banyak (They, We, You).

26. OK Google kunci jawaban untuk kelas 8 buku mandiri bahasa Inggris kurikulum 2013 halaman 106 1practice 2​


Change the verbs in brackets into their past form.

Choosing the appropriate word to fill the blanks is not too difficult if we've known the meaning of each given words.

Based on the text, it is talking about the last school vacation and we will complete the text with the form of past tense.

The simple past tense, sometimes called the preterite, is used to talk about a completed action in a time before now. The time of the action can be in the recent past or the distant past and action duration is not important.

We always use the simple past when we say when something happened, so it is associated with certain past time expressions.

To understand more, let's discuss it.

35. jawaban buku mandiri bahasa inggris kelas 9 halaman 86 practice 1​


23. To mop => Mop => Mopped => Mopping
24. To finish => Finish => Finished => Finishing
25. To trip => Trip => Tripped => Tripping
26. To fall => Fall => Fell => Falling
27. To join => Join => Joined => Joining

PEMBAHASAN
Perintah dari soal di atas adalah mengubah kata kerja to infinitive k dalam bentuk present, past dan continuous (sedang berlangsung).

Present
Dalam present tense kata kerja yang digunakan adalah kata kerja pertama (verb-1), maka untuk mengubah dri kata kerja "to infinitve" ke bentuk present tinggal menghilangkan kata "to".

Present tense adalah tenses yang menjelaskan suatu kebiasaan yang dilakukan sehari-hari dan menjelaskan suatu kebenaran umum.

Contoh :
I am a student.
I think English is easier than math.
Etc.

Past
Dalam past tense kata kerja yang digunakan adalah verb-2, maka untuk mengubah verb-1 dari "present tense" tadi ke dalam verb-2 kita bisa melihat perubahannya dalam regular verbs (kata kerja beraturan) atau pun irregular verbs (kata kerja tidak beraturan).

Past tense adalah tenses yang menjelaskan suatu kegiatan yang dilakukan pada masa lampau.

Berikut ini contoh perubahan verb-1 ke bentuk verb- 2 :
•Go => Went => Gone (Irregular verbs)
•Study => Studied => Studied (Regular verbs)

Contoh kalimat :
•I went to Malang last month.
•They studied English yesterday.
•Etc

Continuous
Dalam continuous tense kata kerja yang digunakan adalah verb-1 di tambahkan dengan -ing.

Contoh :
•Go => Going
•Stay => Staying
•Study => Studying

Continuous tense adalah tenses yang menjelaskan suatu kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung.
Contohnya :
•She is doing her homework.
•We were joining English club.
•Etc
