15. Apa yang dimaksud dengan konsep fade in caption pada editing ?


Jawaban:

fade in : Gambar yang perlahan muncul secara gradual dan biasanya digunakan untuk menandai awal atau akhir. Bisa juga diletakkan di tengah-tengah jika adegan mengalami beat

jadikan jawaban tercerdas

Penjelasan:

Fade in caption dalam editing adalah teknik pengeditan video di mana teks caption (teks yang muncul di layar untuk memberikan informasi atau konteks tentang video) muncul secara perlahan dari keadaan gelap hingga menjadi terlihat secara jelas. Ini memberikan efek yang halus dan tidak mengganggu pada penonton, karena teks tidak muncul secara tiba-tiba dan mengganggu tampilan video yang sedang ditonton.

Dalam praktiknya, fade in caption dilakukan dengan memberikan efek transisi dari hitam ke transparan pada teks caption, sehingga teks muncul secara perlahan dan halus. Teknik ini biasanya digunakan pada awal video, ketika caption pertama kali muncul, atau ketika ada pergantian adegan atau topik yang memerlukan penjelasan tambahan.

21. 3 steps to write photo caption Wild art (definition and example) Punctuation and capitalization in photo caption Photo caption and cutline


Jawaban:

**3 Steps to Write a Photo Caption:**

1. **Describe the Photo:** Begin by providing a concise and clear description of what's happening in the photo. Mention the main subject or action captured in the image.

2. **Add Context:** Give context to the photo by explaining when and where it was taken, who is in the photo, and why it's significant or interesting.

3. **Engage the Audience:** Make the caption engaging by adding a touch of creativity, humor, or emotion. You can also include relevant hashtags or mentions to increase the photo's reach on social media.

**Wild Art (Definition and Example):**

Wild art, often referred to as "wild art stories" in journalism, involves publishing eye-catching, unusual, or offbeat photographs or visuals that may not have a direct connection to a headline news story but are attention-grabbing on their own. These images are often used to break up the text-heavy nature of news publications and captivate the reader's interest.

Example of a Wild Art Caption: "Local artist creates stunning mural on abandoned building, adding a burst of color to the neighborhood."

**Punctuation and Capitalization in Photo Caption:**

- **Punctuation:** Use appropriate punctuation marks like commas, periods, and apostrophes where needed. For example, "Friends enjoying a day at the beach, basking in the sun."

- **Capitalization:** Capitalize the first letter of each sentence and proper nouns (names of people, places, etc.). For example, "Amanda and David explore the historic streets of Paris."

**Photo Caption and Cutline:**

A **photo caption** is a brief description or explanation of a photo, typically found directly beneath or beside the image. It provides context and information about what the viewer is seeing.

A **cutline** is essentially the same as a photo caption and can be used interchangeably. In some cases, "cutline" may be a term more commonly associated with print journalism, while "photo caption" is used in digital and online contexts. Both serve the purpose of helping the audience understand the photo better.

Kolam Vernal

Kolam vernal, atau kolam musiman, adalah jenis habitat lahan basah yang unik. Mereka biasanya kecil, dangkal, badan air fana, dan tidak seperti kolam atau danau, mereka tidak memiliki inlet atau outlet permanen. Mereka diisi setiap musim semi oleh hujan dan salju yang mencair, kemudian mengering untuk jangka waktu tertentu selama musim panas. Kualitas kolam vernal ini membedakannya dari lahan basah lainnya, dan mereka mendukung beberapa spesies hewan yang membutuhkan habitat lahan basah sementara ini untuk bertahan hidup.

Choose a theme that you will use to describe or explain something. 2. After that, choose a picture that will explain or describe what the people in it are doing or saying. 3. Write down your word under or top the picture.  Example of caption: You will have final examination next week. You use a picture to explain what you are doing.
