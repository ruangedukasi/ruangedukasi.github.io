25. bought- via- masks- doni- yesterday- online​


Jawaban:

Doni bought masks via online yesterday.

Pembahasan:

Simple past tense

Simple past tense digunakan utk menyatakan kejadian masa lampau.

Rumus:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contohnyaa..

(+) I met my friend at the market yesterday.

(-) I did not meet my friend at the market yesterday.

(?) Did I meet my friend at the market yesterday?

(+) He sold his house last week.

(-) He did not sell his house last week.

(?) Did he sell his house last week?

32. pak Amir mendorong loker dengan usaha 900 j dengan denangan gay 600 N.Berapakah perpindahan Loker​


Jawaban:

1,5 Meter

Penjelasan:

Diketahui:

Usaha ( W ) = 900 Joule

Gaya ( F ) = 600 N

Ditanyakan:

Perpindahan ( S ) = ........?

Jawab ⤵️

W = F × S

900 = 600 × S

S = 900 : 600

S = 1,5 meter

______________________

W = Usaha ( Joule )

F = gaya ( Newton )

S = perpindahan ( Meter )
