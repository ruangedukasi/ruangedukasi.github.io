4. Cara membuat nugget ayam dalam bahasa Inggris How to make bananas


Jawaban:

How to Make Chicken Nuggets

1. Wash the chicken and cut it into small pieces. Then, blend the chicken meat with the eggs until smooth. You can do this twice, each 250 grams of chicken meat so that the results of the meat can be smoother.

2. Next, pour the results of the blender into a container. Then, add the flour, breadcrumbs, tapioca flour, mashed garlic, salt, pepper, and powdered stock. Stir until all ingredients are evenly mixed.

3. After that, prepare a baking sheet and spread each side using cooking oil. Then, put the dough in a bowl and smooth the surface.

4. If it is flat, steam the nugget dough for about 30 minutes until evenly distributed. You can line the lid of the pot to prevent the steam from dripping onto the nuggets.

5. Once cooked, remove and let the nuggets come to room temperature. Then, cut the nuggets to the size you like.

6. Prepare the dyes and coatings. Put the flour, salt and water in a bowl. Stir until well combined and the mixture is half thickened. Dip the steamed nugget pieces into the mixture you have prepared.

7. When the nuggets are completely covered with a thick batter, roll them in the breadcrumbs while pressing them down. This is so that the breadcrumbs can stick perfectly to the surface of the nuggets.

8. You can immediately fry it on medium heat until the color changes to brownish yellow. However, if you don't want to eat it, you can store it in a closed container and place it in the freezer.

14. uraikan cara membuat olahan chicken nugget​


Jawaban:

Tipe: camilan/lauk

Total Waktu: 2JBahan Resep:

400 gram dada ayam fillet

1 butir telur ayam

3 sdm tepung terigu

3 sdm tepung maizena

1/2 sdm kaldu bubuk

1/2 sdm gula

1/4 sdt merica bubuk

1/4 sdt garam

1 buah wortel, serut dengan serutan keju

2 siung bawang putih (untuk bumbu halus)

3 siung bawang merah (untuk bumbu halus)

1 butir telur, kocok (bahan pelapis)

secukupnya Tepung roti (bahan pelapis)

petunjuk resep :

Langkah 1, campurkan bahan utama nugget ayam: Kecuali serutan wortel dan bahan pelapis, campur semua bahan utama nugget ayam dengan bumbu halus. Campur hingga sempurna.

Langkah 2, campurkan wortel: Apabila bahan utama dan bumbu halus sudah tercampur sempurnya, tambahkan serutan wortel, lalu diaduk lagi menggunakan sendok atau yang lainnya hingga benar-benar rata.

Langkah 3, kukus adonan nugget: Kukus adonan nugget, selama kurang lebih 20 menit saja. Untuk mengecek tingkat kematangan bisa ditusuk menggunakan tusuk gigi, apabila tidak ada lagi adonan yang menempel, berati sudah matang. Angkat lalu diamkan hingga dingin.

Langkah 4, buat adonan pelapis: Siapkan celupan dengan menggunakan telur yang dikocok, dan siapkan pula tepung roti dalam wadah sendiri.

Langkah 5, simpan nugget dalam kulkas sebelum digoreng: Celupkan potongan nugget kedalam kocokan telur, lalu baluri menggunakan tepung roti. Kemudian simpanlah dulu dalam kulkas sekitar 10-15 menit agar tepung roti melekat erat.

Langkah 6, goreng dan sajikan: Panaskan banyak minyak, lalu goreng nugget hingga matang berwarna kecoklatan, angkat, tiriskan lalu sajikan bersama saus sambal dan nasi panas.

materials :
-Medium-size bananas
- 3 tbsp rice flour

- 1/2 tsp vanilla essence 
-2 tbsp milk creamer
- grated cheese 
- Oil for deep-frying
-A pinch of salt and a pinch of sugar
-chocolate sprinkles

Steps :
-First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water. 
-Second, soak bananas in the batter. Heat the oil in a large wok. 
-Third, Drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas.

- Then, pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas. 
- Finally, layer it with grated cheese and sprinkled with chocolate frozen.

33. Ibu membeli daging ayam sebanyak 8,5 kg, sebanyak 2,5 kg daging ayam dibuat ayam goreng. Jika sebanyak 50 % daging ayam yang tersisa dibuat nugget, maka banyaknya ayam yang dibuat nugget adalah . . . Kg.


Diketahui :

a = 8,5 kg

b = 2,5 kg

c = 50%

Ditanya :

x = ?

Maka :

x = (a - b) - c

x = (8,5 - 2,5) - 50%

x = (6) - 50%

x = 3

Jawab:

Jadi daging ayam yang dibuat nugget adalah

3 Kg
