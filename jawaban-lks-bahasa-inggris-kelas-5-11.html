24. Lks bahasa inggris kelas 8 halaman 9-11


The (5) creation (c) of our Solar System took place billions of years before there were any people around to witness it. Our own (6) evolution (h) is tied closely to the evolution of the Solar System. Thus, without understanding from where the Solar System came from, it is difficult to comprehend how mankind came to be (7) made up (a) believe that the Solar System evolved from a giant cloud of dust and gas. They believe that this dust and gas began to collapse under the weight of its own gravity. As it did so, the matter contained within this could begin moving in a giant (8) circle (i) much like the water in a drain moves around the center of the drain in a circle. At the (9) center (e) of this spinning cloud, a small star began to form. This star grew larger and larger as it collected more and more of the dust and gas that collapsed into it. Further away from the center of this mass where the star was forming, there were smaller clumps of dust and gas that were also collapsing. The star in the center eventually ignited forming our Sun, while the smaller clumps became the planets, minor (10) planets (j), moons, comets, and asteroids.

25. Kunci jawaban lks bahasa inggris kelas 5. Hal 18


Berikut bentuk negative dari kalimat tersebut.

1. (+) I read a book yesterday.

(-) I did not read a book yesterday.

2. (+) I went to the market yesterday.

(-) I did not go to the market yesterday.

3. (+) We studied English last week.

(-) We did not study English last week.

4. (+) They played basketball this morning.

(-) They did not play basketball this morning.

5. (+) My mother cooked chicken soup yesterday.

(-) My mother did not cook chicken soup yesterday.

6. (+) Jasmine visited the zoo last holiday.

(-) Jasmine did not visit the zoo last holiday.

7. (+) I was a student.

(-) I was not a student.

8. (+) I did my homework.

(-) I did not do my homework.

9. (+) They were tired.

(-) They were not tired.

10. (+) They were happy.

(-) They were not happy.

Penjelasan:

Simple past tense ialah bentuk kalimat dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Struktur Kalimat

1. Struktur kalimat verbal, struktur kalimat yang predikatnya adalah kata kerja.

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did + not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) He bought a book yesterday.

(-) He did not buy a book yesterday.

(?) Did he buy a book yesterday?

2. Struktur kalimat nominal, struktur kalimat yang predikatnya bukan kata kerja.

(+) S + to be (was/ were) + O

(-) S + to be (was/ were) + not + O

(?) To be (was/ were) + S + O + ?

Contoh kalimat:

(+) She was a doctor.

(-) She was not a doctor.

(?) Was she a doctor?

27. lks bahasa inggris kelas 8


13. no text

14.... his face and style look very vicious. The synonym of the word vicious is... (d) cruel.

Artinya: wajah dan gayanya terlihat sangat bengis.

Penjelasan: kata yang paling mendekati kata bengis/jahat/kejam adalah cruel.

Arrogant = sombong

Coward = pengecut

Greedy = serakah

15. I.... (b) wrote a letter yesterday.

Penjelasan: tanda waktu yesterday menunjukkan peristiwa di masa lalu. Kata 'wrote' paling tepat untuk bentuk simple past tense.

16. This morning, the birds (a) flew to its nest.

Penjelasan: 'this morning' merupakan tanda waktu yg berkaitan dengan peristiwa masa lampu. Kata 'flew' yang paling tepat untuk simple past tense.

17. Last night, I (d) dreamt about something scary.

Penjelasan: 'last night' merupakan tanda waktu yang menunjukkan kejadian di masa lampau. Kata 'dreamt/dreamed' paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat simple past tense.

18. They (c) didn't play football in the field just now.

Penjelasan: 'just now' merupakan tanda waktu yang menunjukkan kejadian yang sudah terjadi, Yang kalimatnya menggunakan simple past tense. Kata 'didn't' paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat negatif dari simple past.

19. Did your brother (b) get married last ten years?

Penjelasan: 'last ten years' menunjukkan tanda waktu kejadian di masa lampau. Kata yang paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat interrogative diatas adalah 'get'.

20. Our friends (d) were happy with their achievement in the competition.

Penjelasan: Kata yang paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas adalah 'were' di mana were digunakan untuk subject jamak dalam bentuk simple past.

Pembahasan

Simple Past Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan tindakan di masa lalu, dan telah selesai di masa lalu.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: yesterday, last week, last month, two days ago, this morning, just now.

BENTUK simple past

    (+) Subject + verb 2  

    (-) Subject + did + NOT + verb 1  

    (?) Did + subject + verb 1 .... ?

Contoh:  

She slept too late last night.I didn't see him yesterday.Did you take my umbrella?What did you do last weekend?
