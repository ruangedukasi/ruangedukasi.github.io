29. matematika kelas 4.jawab yng bnr !​


Jawab:

1. Luas = 18 cm²

  Keliling = 18 cm

2. Luas = 15 cm²

   Keliling = 15 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

1. L = p x l

     = 6 cm x 3 cm

     = 18 cm²

K = 2 x ( p + l )

  = 2 x ( 6 cm + 3 cm )

  = 2 x 9 cm

  = 18 cm

=================================

2. L = ( s x s ) - ( 1 cm x 1 cm )

      = ( 4 cm x 4 cm ) - 1 cm

      = 16 cm² - 1 cm

      = 15 cm²

K = ( 4 x s ) - ( 1 cm x 1 cm )

  = ( 4 x 4 cm ) - 1 cm

  = 16 cm - 1 cm

  = 15 cm

==========================================
